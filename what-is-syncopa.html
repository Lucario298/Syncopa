Syncopa is a deadle disease affecting millions across the world. You might has heard of it, from a friend, a music teacher, or conductor. When a person has syncopa, they have some of the following symptoms:

  • Off-beat rhythm constantly
  • Decrease in musical ability
  • Shortness of breath
  • Hallucination
  • Rarely, a fever
Syncopa is contagious. You can get it from being with others with symptoms and a proper diagnosis. Those who may have symptoms who go untreated may result in:
  • Untreatable lack of rhythm
  • Inability to contuinue musical carrer
  • Death
