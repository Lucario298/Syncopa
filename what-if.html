If you believe you have syncopa, there are several steps you should take.

  1. Refrain from leaving your home until you get a proper diagnosis, as it is contagious.
  2. Go to a syncopist (a doctor who specializes with syncopa and other musical-related illnesses.)

    3. If you do not have syncopa, you can leave home. If you do then:

  3. Undergo tratment. Treatment takes 1-2 weeks. While under treatment, keep your musical ability active. Practice every day, for at least one and a half hours.
